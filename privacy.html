Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information openbwc.org collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It covers only this website. (For the rules that govern using the site, see our Terms of Service.)

Information we collect

We keep data collection to a minimum. There are only two ways we receive information:

  • Usage analytics. We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to see aggregate trends such as which pages are most visited. It does not use cookies and does not collect information that identifies you personally.
  • Messages you send us. When you use our Contact form or email us, we receive what you provide — typically your name, email address, and message.

How we use your information

We use aggregate analytics to improve the website, and we use the messages you send us only to respond to you and keep a record of the conversation.

What we never do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We do not run advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not collect, upload, or process any body-worn camera footage through this website.

Third-party services we rely on

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, uses TelemetryDeck for analytics, and routes contact-form submissions through Formspree. These providers process limited data (such as your IP address, or the details you enter in the form) to deliver the site and pass your message along to us. Each has its own privacy policy governing that data.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above. Questions about your data? Reach us through our Contact page.
